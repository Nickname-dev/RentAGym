Costs and rent

Rent costs consist of several utilities such as electricity, water, and services! It is up to the public to make sure that these utillities are paid for before hand!

The following represent costs for each utility:


    Electricity(lightbulbs) - 15 cents per hour


Water(fountains) - $50 per hour



Custodians(cleaning) - $15 per hour



What is the admission like?

Even though an individual rents a gym for a community event, admission or tickets must be handed out to those taking part in the event. For that reason, there is a box office admission in the main office of the Linn-Mar highschool. Applicants must visit this area of the school to recieve the ticket(ticket for each person).











After the event is issued by the conductor(they can book a day in the Calendar page), must pay the 5 dollar admission fee(per person) to achieve the ticket for the corresponding day.

          










If an individual requests a ticket for an event on October 15th(for example), and if the event is not fully booked, then they have access to ticket admission.



It is up to the 'event organizer' to pay the full fee; directions are given above.
