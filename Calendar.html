Welcome to the Calendar Page!




What is the Calendar Page about? Only for event organizers!


Here, you can book the day which you want to rent the gym! By renting the gym, you have gained access to the facility for the whole day. The time limit allows for preperation, the party, and clean up. Understanding the buttons below is vital for renting the gym. Each button has its own number- a number indicating the day of the month.

                                               

Click on the button the reserve or rent the gym for that day; if you misclick or end up wanting to remove your response, simply refresh the page.                               




                                                                                    School's maintenance

The last ten days of the month are for the school's maintenance, so only the first 20 days are open. Fees after booking can be seen in the planner page; fees are to be paid in the main office.
